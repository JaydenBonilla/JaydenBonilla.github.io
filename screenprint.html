Project Overview

The "Our Shared Canvas" project introduced me to the art of screenprinting. During this project, I learned how to create designs, transfer them to a screen, and print them onto a surface. The process required planning, creativity, and attention to detail.

What I Did

  • Designed artwork for screenprinting.
  • Learned the screen preparation process.
  • Applied ink and printed the final design.
  • Worked carefully to ensure quality results.

What I Learned

I learned how screenprinting combines artistic creativity with technical skills. I also learned the importance of patience, preparation, and precision throughout the printing process.

Skills Developed

This project strengthened my creativity, problem-solving abilities, and attention to detail while teaching me a new artistic technique.
